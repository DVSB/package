Simple, intelligent and secure cloud platform

Secure. Fast. Free & Own.

Samuel Cloud is your own secure solution for scalable and fast cloud storage. Use inexpensive and unlimited file hosting on Amazon S3 with your own domain and yours users.

So, why n200?

  • ✒ Lightweight. Our code is under 1MB. That's why we are so flexibile.
  • 👥 Powerfull User Management. You can controll whole access to your account.
  • 🔗 It's easy to share. One click for Facebook Share, One for Twitter, One for email.
  • ⚙ Our Crazy settings provides you controll on everything! All data about you.
  • 🛇 We use NodeJs 0.10.14 build on V8. The fastest JS core. Faster than Ruby and Python.
  • ⌨ Whole app is super easy to use. We do usability test on every feature.
  • 💻 We use responsive design on every page. You can use tablet and mobile comfortly.
  • 📸 No database. No data about you. No adverts, IPs and ads. We respect your privacy.
  • ◴ We generate free charts about files for you. Only you know about your visitors.
  • 🔒 Cryptography is our strong side. We use SHA3-512 for files, urls, passwords.
  • 🔀 Collaborate with friends. Make your own teams and create your own accounts.
  •  Setup a backup when you want and where you want. Never loose your data again.
  • 🌄 Upload. Backup. Albums. Share. Order. Slideshows. Galleries. We have it all.
  • 🎬 Upload music. Create albums. Share with frends. Make own music world.
  •  Do you want leave? You can remove ALL your data with Easy Drop everytime.
  •  Download you files when you want for free. One click for your custom package of files.
  •  In process of upload we crypting all your files without distribuing you. It's pretty.
  •  We do not store copy of your files. When you trash something it's really trashed.
  •  We handcraft all code. We love Github and open source. We love HTML and Javascript.
  • 🌎 You don't need a US teleph. number or address. We are really global.
  • ❞ We don't steel your rights. If you'll upload photo it's still yours. No copyrights.
